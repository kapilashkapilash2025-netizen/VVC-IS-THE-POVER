About Our College

A Proud Centre of Tamil Education in Vavuniya

Vipulanantha College is a provincial 1AB Tamil-medium mixed school situated in Pandarikulam, Vavuniya, Northern Province, Sri Lanka.

The college provides education from Grade 1 to Grade 13 and supports students through primary, secondary, G.C.E. Ordinary Level and G.C.E. Advanced Level education.

Our school community promotes academic achievement, leadership, cultural identity, discipline, sports, technology and responsible citizenship.
